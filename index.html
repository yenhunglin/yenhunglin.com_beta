Yen-Hung Lin (Jeff)

Yen was born and raised in Taipei, Taiwan, and he graduated from the California College of the Arts with a BFA in Graphic Design. He has 5+ years of design experience, is currently based in SF Bay Area as a Visual designer at Engagio, and previously was a visual, brand, marketing, and UI designer at HTC and MOCACARE. With a graphic, visual, motion, UI, and industrial design background, he has experience working for marketing and product design departments in Healthcare, SaaS, and smartphone hardware companies located in different countries, including the USA and Taiwan.
